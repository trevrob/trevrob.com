About

My name is Trevor Robinson. My mission is to eliminate inefficiencies in order to make lives easier.

As a full stack programmer, I have the ability to create responsive front end designs using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and jQuery. Additionally, I have the ability to build fast, functional, efficient back end codes using PHP, Node.js, and Ruby. I am also proficient with the use of MVC frameworks such as CodeIgniter, Express.js, and Rails. Most of the apps shown below focus on my ability as a back-end programmer, however, I also have significant experience with front-end-heavy work (which I can showcase per request.)
